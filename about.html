Alternate Mom

No, I don't hate men. No, I have nothing against people getting married early or having kids in their 20's. Please don't start throwing rocks at me (Cheez-its are acceptable, though).

So why Alternate Mom?

This advice generator was inspired by the most important person in my life: my Mom. I noticed that a few of my friends had parents that put pressure on them to find a boyfriend ASAP and to get married. I realized how lucky I was that I had a mother who pushed for me to embrace being on my own and to show gratitude for my independence as a woman. She had so many lovely little sayings that I wished my single friends could hear. And so, Alternate Mom was born.

Frandz, no matter what, always know that you are good enough exactly as you are. Don't let society tell you what to do.

Note: Some quotes are my mom's, some are my own, some belong to famous people, and some belong to an independently-minded toucan.
